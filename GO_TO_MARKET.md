# Go To Market

## Product

Job Skill Planner helps job seekers compare target roles, extract skill gaps, and plan what to learn next.

## First Offer

- Free static planner.
- $19 downloadable job-search kit with prompts, trackers, and templates.
- $149 skill-gap audit for job seekers switching careers.

## Buyer

Primary: job seekers, career switchers, bootcamp grads, and workforce-development coaches.

## Positioning

"Paste a role, find the missing skills, and turn the gap into a learning plan."

## Launch Plan

1. Publish the static app.
2. Add examples for data analyst, QA engineer, web developer, and project coordinator roles.
3. Share walkthroughs in job-search, career-change, and bootcamp communities.
4. Offer five free audits for testimonials.

## Revenue Path

Start with audits and downloadable templates. Add API-backed job parsing only after users prove they will pay for the planning workflow.
